# Sample Questions

To help you get started, here are some **Sample Prompts** you can ask in the app:

### **Housing Affordability & Report Analysis**
Prompts focus on housing issues, document filtering, and comparing annual report outcomes. Key functionalities include:

_Sample Questions:_

- What are the main factors contributing to the current housing affordability issues?
- Click one of the **suggested follow-up questions** and get a reasonable response.
- Click the **New topic** button to clear the chat conversation.
- Search for string **Housing Report** and watch the document list become filtered to fewer documents.
- Select two documents in the list chat **(Annual Housing Report 2022 & 2023)** top panel should show **2 Selected** and ask this chat question: **Analyze the two annual reports and compare the positive and negative outcomes YoY.  Show the results in a table.**
![housing](images/readme/dkm_housting.png)
- Click **Details** on the **Annual Housing Report 2023** document to display the pop-up viewer.
- Review the Extractive Summary for accuracy.
- Scroll through pages of the document until pages **10** & **11**.
- Click on **Chat** and ask this question: **“Can you summarize and compare the tables on page 10 and 11?”**. Review the response for nice info. Close the pop-up.
- Search for string **Contracts** and watch the document list become filtered to fewer documents.
- Select **3** to **4** of the **handwritten contract documents**.
- Ask this question: **“Analyze these forms and create a table with all buyers, sellers, and corresponding purchase prices.”**
- Click **Details** button on one of the handwritten contracts.
- Ask in the pop-up chat: **What liabilities is the buyer responsible for within the contract?**

Enables faster insight discovery and smarter data analysis through automated content extraction and chat-based guidance.
